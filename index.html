Yes! Every PawHaul product is designed to work for all dog sizes. The 2-in-1 Walk Bottle comes in 350ml and 550ml, the retractable leash comes in 3m (10ft) and 5m (16ft) lengths, and the LED collar comes in four sizes from Small to XL.

Absolutely — and we recommend it! The 2-in-1 Walk Bottle, collapsible bowl, retractable leash and LED collar are all designed to work together as a complete walk setup. Add them all to your cart and they ship together for free.

    Our Story

    Built For Every Walk

    PawHaul started with one thirsty dog on a hot Saturday afternoon — and a walk that got cut short. Everything we've built since exists so that never happens again.

    01The Walk That Started It All

    It was a hot Saturday afternoon, halfway through a walk, when my dog planted her feet and refused to go another step. She was thirsty, and I had nothing on me but a leash and a poop bag. I ended up cutting the walk short and practically jogging home so she could get to her water bowl.

    That night I kept thinking about how something so simple, just carrying water on a walk, wasn't actually solved by anything on the market. Bulky bowls that spill in your bag. Bottles with nowhere to put food. Nothing built for the walk itself.

    02Building The First Product

    So I built the first PawHaul product: a bottle that holds both water and food in one leak-proof container, with a flip-out spout so your dog can drink in seconds, no bowl, no mess, no cutting the walk short again.

    03Growing Into A Full Lineup

    Once the first product was out, the same pattern kept showing up. A leash that tangled too easily. A poop bag rattling around loose in a pocket. No way to spot your dog after dark. Every product in the PawHaul lineup today exists because a real walk exposed a real problem, not because it looked good on a shelf.

    04Where We're Headed

    PawHaul is still growing, but the mission hasn't changed since that Saturday afternoon: build gear that makes every walk easier, for every dog, every time.
