Got Questions?

How long does shipping take?+
All orders typically arrive within 7-14 business days. You'll receive a tracking number as soon as your order ships so you can follow it the whole way.
Do your products work for all dog sizes?+
Yes! Every PawHaul product is designed to work for all dog sizes. The Walk Kit comes in 350ml and 550ml, the retractable leash comes in 3m (10ft) and 5m (16ft) lengths, and the LED collar comes in four sizes from Small to XL.
Can I buy multiple products together?+
Absolutely — and we recommend it! The Walk Kit, collapsible bowl, retractable leash and LED collar are all designed to work together as a complete walk setup. Add them all to your cart and they ship together for free.
Are the products durable for daily use?+
Every PawHaul product is built for daily walks. The Walk Kit is 100% leak proof, the leashes use heavy-duty nylon, the collars are all-weather resistant, and the collapsible bowl is food-grade silicone that lasts for years.
What is your return policy?+
We offer a 30 day hassle free return policy on all products. Not happy for any reason? We'll make it right — no questions asked.
Does the AirTag holder fit all collars?+
Yes! The PawHaul AirTag holder is universal and clips onto any standard dog collar. It's waterproof and secure so it won't fall off during even the most active walks.

    Our Story

    Built For Every Walk

    PawHaul was created by dog owners who were tired of carrying too much on walks and enjoying them too little. We built the walk gear we couldn't find anywhere else.

    🐕

    Why We Started PawHaul

    Every morning walk used to mean loading up a backpack — a water bottle here, a food container there, poop bags in one pocket, leash in the other, phone somewhere. It was exhausting before the walk even started.

    We looked at everything dog owners were carrying on walks and asked: why isn't there one brand that makes all of this simple, good looking, and actually designed for walks?

    So we built it. PawHaul started with the Walk Kit — water and food in one bottle. Then we added the heavy-duty retractable leash. Then the collapsible bowl, the hands-free running leash, the AirTag holder, the LED light-up collar. More recently we added the dual dog walk coupler for multi-dog households and the walk-clean bag hook so you're never caught without a bag again. Every single product solves a real problem dog owners face on walks every single day.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is simple: make every single walk with your dog easier, safer and more enjoyable — with gear that's actually built for it.

    We believe every product a dog owner uses on a walk should be designed from the ground up for that purpose. Not repurposed. Not generic. Built specifically for walks.

    From the moment you clip on the leash to the moment you get back home — PawHaul has every part of the walk covered.
